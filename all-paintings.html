Church in Alps, Switzerland

This is my newest painting. I interpreted a photograph of a church in Switzerland taken by a photographer and published on Instagram. It took almost a week to complete and you can see the brush strokes that is identical with me. The painting is drawn on a 30 x 30 canvas at 2020.

Weeping Clown

This painting is the my favourite painting. I am impressed by lyrics of "Palyaço" written by Turgut Uyar and decided to draw a weeping clown. This is a replication of another clown painting but it contains my expressions by my brush because I also interpreted the original one. The painting is drawn on a 50 x 60 canvas at 2015.

size: 50 x 70 (cm) | $ 140 (Dollar)
